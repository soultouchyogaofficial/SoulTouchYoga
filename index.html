Guided Practices

Start your mindfulness journey with these gentle exercises. Each practice is designed to bring you back to center, wherever you are.

Grounding Breath (3 min)

Perfect for moments when you need to anchor yourself and release tension.

  1. Find a comfortable seated position with your spine naturally aligned.
  2. Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 counts.
  3. Pause gently at the top for 2 counts.
  4. Release the breath through your nose for 6 counts.
  5. Repeat this cycle 6–8 times, staying present with each breath.
03:00

Body Scan (5 min)

Reconnect with your physical presence and release stored tension.

  1. Gently close your eyes and soften your jaw and shoulders.
  2. Bring awareness to the crown of your head.
  3. Slowly scan downward—face, neck, shoulders, arms, torso, legs, feet.
  4. Notice any sensations without judgment.
  5. With each exhale, imagine releasing tightness and inviting ease.
05:00

Calming Presence (4 min)

A soothing practice to ease anxiety and restore inner peace.

  1. Place one hand over your heart, the other on your belly.
  2. Breathe deeply into your belly, feeling it rise beneath your hand.
  3. Exhale with a soft audible sigh, releasing any worry.
  4. Silently or aloud, repeat: "I am safe. I am here. I am enough."
  5. Continue for 3–5 minutes, returning to the affirmation as needed.
04:00

Note: These practices can be done anytime, anywhere. For the full guided experience, add your own audio tracks to the audio/ folder.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to begin your yoga journey with confidence.

Do I need experience to start?

Not at all! I welcome students of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Each session is tailored to your current ability and goals.

What should I bring to a session?

Just wear comfortable clothing and bring a yoga mat if you have one. For online sessions, ensure you have a quiet space and stable internet connection. I can recommend equipment if needed.

How long are the sessions?

Private sessions are typically 60 minutes, while group classes run for 75-90 minutes. We can customize the duration based on your preferences and schedule.

What are your rates?

Pricing varies by session type. Private sessions start at OMR 25, group classes at OMR 10 per class. Package deals and corporate rates available. Contact me for detailed pricing.

Can I try a class before committing?

Absolutely! I offer a discounted first session so you can experience my teaching style and see if it's the right fit for you. No pressure, no commitment required.

What if I have injuries or health concerns?

Please inform me of any physical limitations or health conditions before we begin. I'll modify poses and create a safe practice that works with your body, not against it.
